# Knowledge

This folder contains structured knowledge sources used by the Swiss blog automation system.

It is intended to hold reusable legal, editorial, and topic-planning materials that support automated or semi-automated content generation, including blog drafting, authority selection, legal explanation, and editorial review.

## Scope

Use this folder for materials concerning:

- Swiss immigration law knowledge
- Swiss nationality law knowledge
- Legal authorities and source packs
- Internal legal research notes
- Website editorial guidance
- Topic maps and content plans
- Structured inputs used by blog generation scripts
- Reusable prompts, guidance, and reference materials

## Folder structure

- `legal_authorities/` — legal authorities, guidance, case law, policy materials, and structured notes organised by legal topic.
- `internal_legal_notes/` — internal explanatory notes, legal analysis, practical observations, and reusable research summaries.
- `website_editorial/` — editorial rules, tone guidance, formatting standards, SEO guidance, and website-specific content instructions.

Other knowledge folders may be added where a distinct category of reusable material is needed.

## Key files

- `authority_pack_map.json` — maps topics, routes, or issues to relevant legal authority packs.
- `topics.json` — structured topic list used for content planning and blog generation.

## Inclusion criteria

Add material here only if it is likely to be reused by the blog automation workflow or by legal/editorial content production.

Do not add one-off working notes, draft outputs, or generated blog posts here unless they have lasting value as a knowledge source.

Where a document could fit in more than one folder, place it in the most specific applicable location and cross-reference it elsewhere if needed.

## File naming

Use clear, descriptive filenames.

Suggested formats:

`legal_authority_[topic]_[jurisdiction_or_source]_[short_description]`

`internal_note_[topic]_[short_description]`

`editorial_guidance_[purpose_or_page_type]`

Examples:

`legal_authority_family_reunification_swiss_spouse_children_framework_pack`

`internal_note_c_permit_absences_continuous_residence`

`editorial_guidance_swiss_immigration_blog_style`
