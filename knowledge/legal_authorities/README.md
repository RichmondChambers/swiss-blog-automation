# Legal Authorities

This folder contains legal authorities, policy materials, guidance, case law, structured notes, and authority packs used to support Swiss immigration and nationality law research, advice, drafting, and content production.

## Scope

Use this folder for materials concerning:

- Swiss immigration law
- Swiss nationality law
- Residence permits
- Settlement / C permits
- Family reunification
- Route-specific eligibility rules
- Issue-specific legal or factual questions
- Administrative guidance and policy materials
- Tribunal, court, and authority decisions
- Structured legal research notes

## Folder structure

- `family_reunification/` — authorities relevant to family reunion and dependant applications.
- `settlement_and_c_permit/` — authorities relevant to settlement and Swiss C permit applications.

Additional topic folders may be added where a distinct area of law requires its own authority base.

## Inclusion criteria

Add a document here only if it is a legal authority, official guidance, policy source, case law reference, structured legal note, or authority pack that is likely to be reused in legal research, drafting, advice, or content production.

Where a source is relevant to multiple legal topics, place it in the most specific applicable folder and cross-reference it elsewhere if needed.

## File naming

Use clear, descriptive filenames.

Example:

`legal_authority_family_reunification_swiss_spouse_children_framework_pack`
