# Broad Settlement and C Permit Authorities

This folder contains high-level materials, policy overviews, background sources, and general legal frameworks relevant to settlement and C permit matters.

## Scope

Use this folder for materials concerning:

- General settlement and C permit policy
- Background legal frameworks
- High-level summaries of residence and settlement law
- General administrative practice
- Overview materials from federal or cantonal authorities
- Comparative or contextual materials that support understanding of the wider settlement regime

## Inclusion criteria

Add a document here if it gives broad legal, policy, or practical context for settlement and C permit work, but is not specific enough to belong in `core/`, `route_specific/`, or `issue_specific/`.

Where a source is central and repeatedly relied upon, place it in `core/` instead.

## File naming

Use clear, descriptive filenames.

Example:

`legal_authority_broad_swiss_settlement_policy_overview`
